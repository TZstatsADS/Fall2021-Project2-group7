COVID-19 testing access for children at NYC Health + Hospitals

Children who are 2yrs of age or older can be tested at any of the sites listed below. Children younger than 2yrs of age
can be tested at any of the sites listed in blue below. All saliva self testing sites are
for 4 years old and above.

Walk-in testing is available at no cost to you at these NYC Health + Hospitals locations.
